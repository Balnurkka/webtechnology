About Us

Welcome to Nami No Yoru

Opened in 2009. The creation of Nani no Yoru involves the idea of combining two unique features of Japanese culture. On the one hand, the special technology for preparing sushi and rolls strictly follows Japanese traditions.

Оn the other hand, the non-standard character of manga comics fills the menu with new, bold trends, turning it into a colorful holiday of taste.
